About Gina

Gina Kalagaropolos is a contemporary Greek artist painting on the island of Trizonia. Her work captures the luminous Ionian light, quiet coastal moments, and the warmth of small island life through expressive oil and mixed-media paintings.

Working daily in her seaside studio, Gina creates original artworks rooted in memory and place. She ships directly from Greece to global collectors and offers custom commissions inspired by your favorite landscapes and scenes.

Gina in her Trizonia studio

Artist Statement

“I paint to keep the Ionian light alive. Each painting is a memory of Trizonia’s sea, sky, and village life.”
