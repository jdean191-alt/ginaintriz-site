Commissions

Create something just for you

If you’d like a piece of art made especially for your home, your story, or someone you love, I’d be delighted to create a custom artwork inspired by the colours and magic of Trizonia.

I work with resin, texture, gold leaf, and mixed media, bringing to life the sea creatures, island scenes, and whimsical characters that define my style. Whether you’re dreaming of an octopus swirling through turquoise waves, a crab full of personality, a window looking out to the Aegean, or something entirely your own, we can bring it to life together.

How commissions work

1. Share your idea

Tell me what you have in mind — the subject, colours, mood, or a piece of mine you love. You don’t need to know all the details; we can shape it together.

2. Choose your size

I offer a range of canvas sizes, from small keepsakes to large statement pieces. If you’re unsure what size suits your space, I can help you choose.

3. I create your artwork

Once we agree on the concept and size, I begin the piece. Resin artworks take time to layer and cure, so please allow for a little patience — the results are worth it.

4. Final approval

Before shipping, I’ll send you photos of the finished artwork to make sure you’re completely happy.

5. Delivery

Your piece will be carefully packaged and shipped from Trizonia to your door.

Pricing

Commission prices vary depending on size, materials, and complexity. Once you share your idea, I’ll provide a clear quote with no surprises.
